Installation Guide

Follow this simple step by step guide to install first a wallpaper image and then custom icons on your mobile. If you prefere a video, search for 'ios 14 shortcuts icons' (e.g. this video)

German version is available here. Die Anleitung in deutscher Version ist hier zu finden.

#1. Place the downloaded images into your Photos app.

#2. Tap on the share icon.

#3. Tap on 'Use as Wallpaper'.

#4. Position the image and tap on 'Set' and then 'Set Both' to activate the wallpaper.

#5. Go back to your Homescreen, find the 'Shortcuts' App and open it.

#6. Tap the '+' icon on the top to add a new shortcut.

#7. Tap the '+ Add Action' button to add a new action to this shortcut

#8. Find and tap 'Open App' (use the text field)

#9. Tap the 'Choose' field and select the app you want to change the icon of

#10. Tap the 'More' (three dots) button on top

#11. Tap 'Add to Home Screen'.

#12. Tap the placeholder icon and select 'Choose Photo'. Find and select the desired icon image. Optionally rename the app as you want it to appear

#13. Tap 'Add'

#14. Tap 'Done'. The new app icon is now created

#15. Your new App Icon should now be on the home screen. Repeat step 6-14 for every app you want to change.
